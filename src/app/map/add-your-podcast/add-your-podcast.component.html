Add a podcast

We’d love to have more podcasts on Podmap! Here’s how to add one. Please only add it if you know for sure where it’s produced.

  1. Search it
  2. Enter the city where it’s produced. Multiple cities are allowed.
  3. Wait for it to be moderated.
  4. Once approved, it will appear on the map!

The granularity of location is confined to cities, so where you may want to enter "Brooklyn, NY" as a location, you’ll have to enter New York. Though this is slightly less accurate, it allows for better aggregation.

Location suggestions are usually moderated once per week on Fridays.
